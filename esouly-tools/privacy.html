How Our Tools Work

All tools on eSouly Tools process your files and data entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. Your files never leave your device.

We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used solely for analytics and improving the service.

Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to remember your preferences (dark mode, language). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Advertising
